# Code of Conduct

ParetoProof aims to be a serious, high-trust research collaboration.

## Expectations

- Be precise, charitable, and evidence-seeking.
- Critique ideas, proofs, implementations, and benchmark design choices without attacking people.
- When you disagree, state the technical disagreement and the evidence you would accept.
- Share failures and negative results honestly; benchmark quality depends on it.
- Keep public discussion accessible to newcomers without lowering the technical bar.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or sustained hostility.
- Knowingly misrepresenting benchmark results, artifacts, or provenance.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Spamming, trolling, or derailing technical threads.

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, project-board communication, and linked community spaces used for ParetoProof work.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, contact the repository maintainer privately through GitHub or another trusted channel. Reports will be handled as promptly and discreetly as possible.
