# Governance

ParetoProof starts as a maintainer-led project with a strong bias toward transparent technical decisions.

## Current model

- The repository owner acts as the initial maintainer.
- Major benchmark, infrastructure, and policy changes should be proposed in Discussions or pull requests.
- Architecture decisions that affect downstream work should be captured in `docs/adrs/`.

## Maintainer responsibilities

- Curate the benchmark scope and public roadmap.
- Keep benchmark provenance and scoring policy explicit.
- Review or delegate review for contributions that change methodology.
- Protect the integrity of published results and artifacts.

## How decisions are made

- Small changes: issue or pull request discussion.
- Cross-cutting changes: discussion thread plus ADR or merged design doc update.
- Urgent operational changes: maintainer call, followed by a written summary in GitHub.

## Future direction

If the project grows into a broader research organization or lab, this repository can be transferred into a future `ParetoResearch` GitHub organization without changing its operating model.
